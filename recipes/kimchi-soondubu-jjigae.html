Kimchi Soondubu Jjigae

Description

Soondubu jjigae is made with ripe kimchi and thinly sliced meat. This surprisingly hearty stew is quick and easy. Try making this at home!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz extra soft/silken tofu

  • 1/3 cup thinly sliced kimchi

  • 3 oz pork or beef

  • 1-3 tsp red chili pepper flakes

  • 1-3 tsp gochugaru

  • 1 tsp minced garlic

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil

  • 1 cup water or anchovy broth

  • 2-3 tbsp kimchi juice

  • 1/2 tsp saewujeot (salted shrimp)

  • pinch black pepper

  • 1 scallion, finely chopped

  • 1 egg

Cooking Directions

  1. Cut the meat and kimchi into thin strips.

  2. Add the kimchi, meat, red chili pepper, garlic, and sesame oil to a small pot and place over medium heat.

  3. stir-fry until meat is almost cooked, 3-4 minutes

  4. Pour in water/broth and the kimchi juice. Bring it to a boil and continue to boil for 3-4 minutes. (skim off the scum)

  5. Add the soft tofu in big chunks. Stir in the salted shrimp and black pepper. Cook for 4-5 minutes, then add the chopped scallion just before removing the pot from the stove.

  6. Crack 1 egg in to the stew while it's still hot.
